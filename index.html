Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our standard minimum order starts from 10 to 20 Kilograms. However, we are fully capable of handling large-scale wholesale and container shipments as seen in our warehouse capacity.
Do you sell both raw and roasted coffee beans?
Yes. We primarily supply raw (green) Robusta and Arabica beans from Sumatra in large quantities. We also offer roasted coffee beans that can be adjusted to your specific classification.
What other plantation products do you supply?
Apart from Coffee and Clove, we also supply betel nut, nutmeg, mace, nutmeg essential oil, vanilla, black pepper, and raw rubber. All with guaranteed export quality.
